How to use Medidoro:
1. Press play to begin 1st, 25 minute productivity session.
2. After productivity session click "5 minutes" and select
your relaxing environment of choice.
3. At end of 5 minutes select "25 minutes" and your
productivity environment of choice.
4. Continue this cycle to complete 4 successful
productivity sessions.

P.S. We reccomend selecting "coffee" for productivity
because it keeps you on the...
GRIND...pun intended.
